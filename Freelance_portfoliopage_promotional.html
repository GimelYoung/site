Flyers
I have created numerous flyers, for events and meetings. Each flyer created came in multiple design variations. Here are some examples.

School Reward Dollars
As part of an initiative to promote and reinforce positive student behavior, the school incorporated a reward system. I designed the reward currency using the schools logo and various images of the school’s patron George Westinghouse.
I was later tasked with creating more reward currency for a second school. For this project I used the Eagle Academy for Young Men School’s coat of arms and the school’s C.L.E.A.R. principal idea.
